Industrial laser systems manufacturer with 15+ years of experience serving manufacturers worldwide. Quality machines, professional support, lasting partnerships.

Our Story

Committed to Manufacturing Excellence

HG Star Technology has been designing and manufacturing industrial laser equipment since 2010. From our headquarters in Jinan, China — the world's laser equipment capital — we produce a complete range of fiber laser cutting, welding, cleaning and marking systems.

Our SMART series machines are engineered for reliability, precision and long-term productivity. We serve clients in over 60 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, Africa and South America, providing not just machines but complete manufacturing solutions including installation, training and after-sales support.

Every machine undergoes rigorous quality testing before shipment. We use only certified components from leading brands including Raycus, IPG, Beckhoff, Yaskawa and SMC to ensure consistent performance and easy maintenance.
